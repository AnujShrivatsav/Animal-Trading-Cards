Panda

Panda is only found in China and behaves like a human baby. It is the largest living herbivore.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda Melanoleuca
  • Average Length: 180cm
  • Average Lifespan: 45 years
  • Habitat: Tropical Trees

Panda is a herbivorous animal native to the South Central China. It is characterised by large, black patches around it's eyes, over the ears, and across it's round body. The name "Giant panda" is sometimes used to distuinguish it from the red panda.
